Brand pair chosen:
Zomato -> Swiggy

Key design decisions:
- Color: Zomato’s bold red/orange for urgency & appetite; Swiggy’s orange for friendly, approachable feel.

- Typography: Bold, large headings to highlight offers and actions quickly.

- Layout: Simple, card-based UI for fast scanning and easy ordering.

- CTA Placement: Clear buttons like Order Now and Continue to reduce friction.

- Imagery: High-quality food visuals to trigger cravings and build trust.
